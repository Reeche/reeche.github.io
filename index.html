Ruiqi He

ruiqi.he@outlook.com

PhD reseacher at the Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems in Tübingen, Germany.

My research lies in the intersection between machine learning and cognitive science, where I design and conduct online experiments and apply machine learning frameworks to model and understand the underlying mechanism behind metacognitive learning in the context of planning.

My research interest include cognitive modelling, reinforcement learning, multi-agent systems and game theory.

Apart from doing research, I enjoy most of my time travelling and cooking.
